How OL Maths is marked

Method marks
Using the right approach — even if the final answer is wrong. Writing down the correct formula gets you method marks. These are the most important marks to chase.
Substitution marks
Correctly putting the right values into the formula. Even if you get the arithmetic wrong afterward, substitution marks are awarded.
Answer marks
Getting the correct final answer. These are worth less than method marks — never leave an answer blank just because you're unsure.
The golden rule
Always show your working. A correct answer with no working can get zero. Working with a wrong answer can get most of the marks. Write every step.
